Data Sources

Primary Data

Happiness Points Collected via survey from Drexel University students as part of urban experience research. Students identified locations in Philadelphia that evoked feelings of happiness.

Google Street View Imagery Accessed via Google Street View API. Panoramic images (3328 × 1664 pixels) downloaded using tile-based retrieval method at zoom level 3.

  • Google. (2024). Street View Static API. https://developers.google.com/maps/documentation/streetview

U.S. Census Bureau American Community Survey 5-year estimates (2018-2022) at census tract level for Philadelphia County.

  • U.S. Census Bureau. (2023). American Community Survey 5-Year Data (2018-2022). https://www.census.gov/programs-surveys/acs

Philadelphia Road Network OpenDataPhilly street centerlines dataset.

  • City of Philadelphia. (2024). Street Centerlines. https://opendataphilly.org/datasets/street-centerlines/
